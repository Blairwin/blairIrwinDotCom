Voice Actor

Hi, my name is Cole. I'm a Voice Actor, based in Toronto, Ontario.

I am 5 years old (almost 6!).

I love subways, trains, buses, and memorizing maps. I also love to read and listen to podcasts. Charlie and the Chocolate Factory is my favourite book right now and Smash Boom Best is my favourite podcast at the moment.

One day, when my Mummy was auditioning for a new animated show, I said "But I want to be the voice of that character!" and my Mummy talked to me about it and we decided that I could start auditioning for fun voice work too! It is so much fun!
